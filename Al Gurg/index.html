ABOUT

Siematic

Customers worldwide recognize the brand SieMatic for its premium-kitchens of first-class, outstanding design along with the highest quality of workmanship. Since 1929 SieMatic is manufacturing first-class kitchen furniture – and nothing else. Our kitchens have been exported since 1960 and today are the embassadors of German brand-name quality in more than 60 countries worldwide.

PURE

collection

The SieMatic PURE style collection opens up a cornucopia of creative options, with room concepts integrating the functions of the kitchen with the architecture, perfectly and unobtrusively. The elegant minimalistic language of form of the furniture elements from the SieMatic PURE style collection places the focus on what really counts. Learn more.

URBAN

collection

It is the unconventional, intuitive juxtaposition of carefully selected objects and thematic contrasts: exciting and peaceful, strange and familiar, modest and ostentatious, flea market find and design classic. It is “solitaire” planning in which each object has a relationship with its neighbor. Learn more.

CLASSIC

collection

Composing is an art – and that also applies to interior design. With this in mind, SieMatic works together with renowned international designers to develop room design concepts for the style collection Classic, whose options go far beyond traditional kitchen design and offer broad latitude for interpretation. Learn more.
